Previous Experience:

Aqua Environment, Inc.

San Rafael, CA || June - August 2015

Engineering internship focused on modernizing the manufacturing process of their products with a focus on computer aided design. Using Autodesk Inventor CAD software, I created 3-dimensional models of pieces that Aqua Environment makes as part of their high-pressure regulators, to be referenced by employees when making the regulators. Additionally, I helped create a back-pressure regulator testing system to make employee testing of back-pressure regulators easier and standardized. This involved combining an Arduino with a self-made circuit board in conjunction with the regulators.

Teacher's Assistant

Amherst College || January 2016 - Present

Teaching undergraduate students in the course Introduction to Computer Science I to write complex programs in Java.

Tutor

September 2011 - June 2015

Through the company Marin Tutors, I was paired with high school students around Marin who needed tutoring help. I worked particularly with students who needed help in math and science, subjects ranging from Algebra I and II, Geometry, Precalculus, Calculus, General Science, Chemistry, and Physics.

Peer Resources

Marin Academy || September 2013 - June 2015

One of eighteen students selected from competitive applicant pool to join student group on campus that met weekly for training in facilitation and leadership skills, as well as event planning for the greater community. Responsibilities included planning and hosting speakers, open student meetings, panel discussions, and seminars with the goal of facilitating dialogue between parents, students, and faculty. Also held special programs to assist freshmen and new students transition to Marin Academy. Primary event each year was Communications Evening, an evening event attended by roughly 250 students and parents; members of Peer Resources performed monologues about high school life, and then facilitated discussions with groups of 20 students and parents.
